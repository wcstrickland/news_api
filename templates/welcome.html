Article format

The articles contained in this database consist of:
1. The date they were published.
2. The topic
3. The "headline"
4. An expanded headline labeled the summary.
5. The url to the article

Searching the database

The API allows you to search the database in a number of ways:
*By time frame of publishing.
*By occurrence of a key word in a particular field.
*A combination of the two.

For example you could search for all of the articles from November 14th - November 21st where the headline included the word emergency.

Furthermore, you can filter the out put of your search to provide only selected fields.
The example search described previously could be tailored to yield only the URL for the resulting articles.

Searching by date

To search by date:
Follow the url to this page with /daterange/"From date"/"To date"

An example would look like:
http://127.0.0.1:5000/daterange/2020-10-31/2020-11-10
This would provide all articles published between Oct. 31st and Nov. 10th.

Searching by keyword

To search by keyword:
Follow the url to this page with /key/"The part of the headline you want to search"/"The key word you are looking for"

An example would look like:
http://127.0.0.1:5000/key/topic/Joe
This would provide all articles where the word "Joe" appeared in the "Topic"

Combining date a search

To combine the two:
Follow the url to this page with /keyrange/"From date"/"To date"/"The part of the headline you want to search"/"The key word you are looking for"

An example would look like:
http://127.0.0.1:5000/keyrange/2020-10-31/2020-11-10/topic/Joe
This would provide all articles where the word "Joe" appeared in the "Topic" during the specified range.

Filtering Results

To filter result output:
Follow the url to this page with /specific/"desired field"/"From date"/"To date"/"The part of the headline you want to search"/"The key word you are looking for"

An example would look like:
http://127.0.0.1:5000/specific/url/2020-10-31/2020-11-10/topic/Joe
This would provide just the url's for all articles where the word "Joe" appeared in the "Topic" during the specified range.

Word clouds

When searching by either date or by key word the resulting list of articles
can have their content combined and a word cloud
visualization of the most common words produced.
This excludes "stop words" such as "the", "but", "and" etc.

At the end of a search simply add /cloud.
http://127.0.0.1:5000/key/topic/Joe/cloud
